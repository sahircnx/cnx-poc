# Promo Cards Block

The Promo Cards block displays a row of call-to-action cards with images on the left and content on the right.

## Authoring Pattern

Each row in the block table represents one card.

| Image                             | Content                                                      |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| ![Thumbnail](/path/to/image.jpg)  | ## PROTECT YOUR CHILD'S FUTURE <br> [GET TO KNOW MORE](link) |
| ![Thumbnail](/path/to/image2.jpg) | ## PAY YOUR LAST RESPECTS <br> [GET TO KNOW MORE](link)      |

## Implementation Details

- **Layout**: Flexbox-based grid. 3 columns on desktop, stacking on mobile.
- **Design**: Rounded corners (`10px`), thin blue border (`#28aae1`).
- **Typography**: Roboto Extra Bold (900) for deep blue headings.
- **Button**: Nico Life light blue (`#28aae1`) with white bold text.
